Introduction & Scope

Welcome to SAT Score Calculator. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational tool at https://satscorecalculator.io/. Our service is an independent educational calculator that estimates SAT scores and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the College Board.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • General location information (country/region level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referral sources

Calculator Inputs

When you use our SAT score calculator:

  • All calculations are processed locally in your browser
  • Your score inputs are not transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • No user accounts or personal information are required or collected

Cookies & Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve our service.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze website performance and user engagement
  • Improve our calculator features and user experience
  • Monitor site security and prevent abuse
  • Generate aggregate statistics about site usage

Ads/Monetization

We may show advertisements in the future (e.g., Google AdSense) to help support the free operation of our educational tool. If implemented, we will update this privacy policy accordingly and ensure compliance with applicable advertising privacy standards.

Data Retention & Security

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' data retention policies. We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect the information we collect, though no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You have several options to control your privacy:

  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics
  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Children's Privacy

Our site is designed for students preparing for the SAT, including those under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our calculator processes all inputs locally without storing personal data on our servers.

International Transfers

Our website uses standard cloud services and analytics tools that may transfer data internationally. These services comply with applicable data protection regulations and frameworks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
