Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 18, 2025

Introduction & Scope

Welcome to SAT Score Calculator. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational tool at https://satscorecalculator.io/. Our service is an independent educational calculator that estimates SAT scores and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the College Board.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • General location information (country/region level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Referral sources

Calculator Inputs

When you use our SAT score calculator:

  • All calculations are processed locally in your browser
  • Your score inputs are not transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • No user accounts or personal information are required or collected

Cookies & Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and provide analytics. We categorize cookies into the following types:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of essential cookies.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website through Google Analytics 4, collecting anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.
  • Advertising Cookies: May be set by our advertising partners (such as Google AdSense) to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.
  • Personalization Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your language preference and customizing your experience.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Consent Management & Your Control

We respect your privacy choices and provide you with full control over non-essential cookies. When you first visit our website, you'll see a consent banner allowing you to manage your cookie preferences.

Your Consent Options

  • Accept All: Consent to all cookie categories including analytics, advertising, and personalization cookies.
  • Reject All: Only essential cookies will be used. Analytics, advertising, and personalization cookies will be blocked.
  • Customize: Choose which specific cookie categories you want to allow. You can enable or disable each category individually.

Managing Your Consent

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Manage Consent" button below
  • Using your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Clearing your browser's local storage to reset your choices

Google Consent Mode v2

We implement Google Consent Mode v2, which ensures that Google services (Analytics and AdSense) only operate according to your consent choices. This means:

  • If you deny consent, analytics and advertising cookies will not be set
  • Google services will operate in a privacy-enhanced mode when consent is denied
  • Your choices are stored locally in your browser and persist across sessions
  • You can withdraw consent at any time using the "Manage Cookie Preferences" button above
  • We comply with GDPR and other international privacy regulations

Cookie Retention & Data Storage

Your consent choices are stored in your browser's local storage and will be remembered for future visits. If you clear your browser data or use a different device, you will need to set your preferences again. We do not track your consent choices across devices.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze website performance and user engagement
  • Improve our calculator features and user experience
  • Monitor site security and prevent abuse
  • Generate aggregate statistics about site usage

Ads/Monetization

We may show advertisements in the future (e.g., Google AdSense) to help support the free operation of our educational tool. If implemented, we will update this privacy policy accordingly and ensure compliance with applicable advertising privacy standards.

Data Retention & Security

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' data retention policies. We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect the information we collect, though no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You have several options to control your privacy:

  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics
  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Children's Privacy

Our site is designed for students preparing for the SAT, including those under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our calculator processes all inputs locally without storing personal data on our servers.

International Transfers

Our website uses standard cloud services and analytics tools that may transfer data internationally. These services comply with applicable data protection regulations and frameworks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
